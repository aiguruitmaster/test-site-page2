Account Verification (KYC) Questions

Account verification, also known as Know Your Customer (KYC), is a regulatory requirement that helps Bet Ninja Casino maintain a safe and secure gaming environment. This process prevents fraud, money laundering, and underage gambling while protecting your account from unauthorized access. All licensed online casinos are required to perform KYC checks to comply with international gaming regulations.

What documents do I need for verification?

To verify your account at Bet Ninja Casino, you'll typically need to provide:

  • A government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card)
  • Proof of address dated within the last three months (utility bill, bank statement, or official government correspondence)
  • Proof of payment method (photo of your credit/debit card or screenshot of e-wallet account)

How long does the verification process take?

The KYC verification process at Bet Ninja Casino usually takes between 24 to 72 hours. Our dedicated team works diligently to review documents as quickly as possible. To avoid delays, ensure all documents are clear, valid, and show all four corners of the document. You'll receive an email notification once your account has been successfully verified.
