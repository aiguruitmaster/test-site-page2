Understanding Wagering Requirements

Wagering requirements are a crucial aspect of any casino bonus, and understanding them is essential for making informed decisions about which promotions to claim at Bet Ninja Casino.

What Are Wagering Requirements?

Wagering requirements, also called playthrough or rollover requirements, specify how many times you must wager the bonus amount (or bonus plus deposit) before you can withdraw any winnings derived from the bonus funds. For example, a $100 bonus with a 35x wagering requirement means you need to place $3,500 in bets before withdrawal is permitted.

Game Contributions

Not all games contribute equally toward meeting wagering requirements at Bet Ninja Casino. Slots typically contribute 100%, meaning every dollar wagered counts fully toward the requirement. Table games like blackjack and roulette often contribute less, sometimes 10-20%, while some games may not contribute at all. Always check the specific terms for each promotion to understand which games are optimal for clearing wagering requirements.

Time Limits

Bonuses at Bet Ninja Casino come with expiration dates. You must meet the wagering requirements within the specified timeframe, typically ranging from 7 to 30 days, or the bonus and any associated winnings will be forfeited. Planning your gameplay to ensure you can realistically meet these requirements is an important consideration before claiming any bonus.

Maximum Bet Limits

When playing with bonus funds, Bet Ninja Casino enforces maximum bet limits to prevent bonus abuse. Exceeding these limits while wagering with bonus funds may result in the bonus and winnings being voided. These limits are clearly stated in the terms and conditions of each promotion.
